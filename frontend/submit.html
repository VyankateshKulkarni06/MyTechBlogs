Submission FAQ

Common questions about our submission process

We typically review submissions within 3-5 business days. Complex technical articles might take slightly longer as we ensure accuracy and quality. You'll receive an email notification once we've made a decision.

Currently, we don't offer monetary compensation, but we provide excellent exposure and promotion for your work. Your articles get featured across our social media channels, newsletter, and website, helping you build your professional brand.

We only accept original, unpublished content. However, if you've published something on your personal blog with limited reach, please contact us directly to discuss. We may consider it on a case-by-case basis.

If your submission doesn't meet our current needs, we'll provide constructive feedback on how to improve it. You're welcome to revise and resubmit, or submit different content in the future. Rejection doesn't mean your writing isn't good!
